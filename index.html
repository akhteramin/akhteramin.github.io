I am an PhD Student of Computing and Information Science at the Rochester Institute of Technology. I am a member of the Accessibility group at the CAIR(Center for Accessibility and Inclusion Research) Lab. I am also a graduate research assistant the supervision of Dr. Matt Huenerfauth at RIT.

My research prospect is in computer accessibility for people who are Deaf or Hard of Hearing, assistive technology for people with disabilities, human computer interaction, and computational linguistics. My prime research space is caption technology, metric and usability.

​

I studied Computer Science at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET). My prior research interest was Quantum Computation and I was an Undergraduate Research Assistant at Next Generation Computing Group under supervision of Dr. Alim Al Islam.
